Credit/Lending

Credit and lending platforms in the DeFi ecosystem allow users to borrow and lend cryptocurrencies. They typically require borrowers to provide collateral in the form of cryptocurrencies, and lenders earn interest on their deposits. Examples of popular credit/lending platforms include Aave, Compound, and MakerDAO.

Credit/Lending protocols are among the highest for TVL on chain, with Aave and Benqi alone responsible for over half the TVL on all of Avalanche.
