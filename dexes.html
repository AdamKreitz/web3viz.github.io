DEXs

Decentralized exchanges (DEXs) are online platforms that enable peer-to-peer cryptocurrency trading without the need for a central authority or intermediary. Unlike centralized exchanges, which are controlled by a single entity, DEXs allow users to transact directly with one another in a trustless, permissionless environment.

By using smart contracts and blockchain technology, DEXs eliminate the need for middlemen such as banks or brokers, resulting in lower transaction fees and increased security. Because they are decentralized, DEXs are also more resistant to censorship and hacking attempts.

However, DEXs can be more complex and less user-friendly than centralized exchanges, and may have lower liquidity and slower transaction speeds. Nonetheless, they offer an alternative option for those who value privacy, security, and the principles of decentralization in their trading activities.
